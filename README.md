# Olist E-commerce Logistics Analysis 

## Project Overview
This project analyzes the logistics performance of **Olist**, the largest department store marketplace in Brazil. The goal was to identify bottlenecks in the delivery process and measure their direct impact on customer satisfaction.

## Key Business Insights
* **The Logistics Bottleneck:** Carrier transit time accounts for **74% of total delivery time** (9.28 days average).
* **Regional Disparity:** A significant delivery gap exists between the Core Market (**Sao Paulo: 5.5 days**) and Northern regions like **Roraima (25.6 days)**.
* **Satisfaction Impact:** There is a clear correlation between delivery delays and lower ratings. States with transit times over 20 days consistently show average scores **below 4.0/5.0**.
